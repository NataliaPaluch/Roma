Ski slopes near Rome

Ski slopes in Rome and the surrounding area?


Few remember that Rome is surrounded by mountains, and in the winter they mean "snow and fun." All Ski Europe makes towards the Alps, a few are aware that in the central and southern Italy, you can find very favorable conditions for winter sports. The ski season takes a little less than in the north of the country, but it usually lasts long enough to encourage many to skiers. For good ski stations of Rome can be reached by car in an hour and a few minutes. Some centers even offer special packages with ski-bus from Rome, meals and discounts for rental of ski equipment.

Ski slopes in the vicinity of Rome: Terminillo (Rieti), Campo Felice (L'Aquila), Ovindoli (Avezzano), Campo Imperatore (Gran Sasso), Campo Staffi (Fiuggi), Monte Livata (Subiaco), Roccaraso (Abruzzo).
