Trevi Fountain ...

... One of the most romantic places in Rome!

Among the narrow streets constantly crowded center of the Eternal City is one of the most popular places in the capital - a small square with a huge fountain ...

Trevi Fountain is one of the most beautiful and impressive buildings of the Italian water. This stunning Baroque fountain located on one whole wall of the palace Poli and is 20 meters wide and 26 meters high. The place is full of charm not only for its size, but also thanks to the reflexes sun on rippling surface of water in the basin pool.

World famous Trevi Fountain owes not only the unique charm and the custom of throwing her pieniążków (as you throw it once you come back!), But also the Swedish actress Anita Ekberg, who in the film Federico Fellini's La Dolce Vita (sweet life) consumed here nightly bath. This one of the most famous scenes in the history of cinema there are still many followers followers. However, it is strictly prohibited and punishable by a high mandate.
