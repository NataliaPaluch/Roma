CINECITTA’ WORLD

Each of Italy (and many fans of cinema in the world!) Knows what is the name of Cinecitta '- Roman town film. It is here that formed the most important works of Italian cinema and numerous megahity world cinema. Today, the town declined slightly, but still tries to exploit commercially his former splendor. Cinecittà Studios can be visited, and their "copy" in the village of podrzymskiej CASTEL ROMANO (Pomezia) can also be ideal to play. From July 2014 began its activity since the amusement park Cinecitta World dedicated to the fascinating world of cinema.

Designed by three-time Academy Award winner Dante Ferretti and full of music Oscar winner Ennio Morricone (MISSION watched with Robert de Niro?), Cinecittà World offers its guests an exciting entertainment classic amusement park combined with discovering the world of film. At the park visitors waiting more than 20 attractions (the largest in Italy rollercaster ALTAIR!) 8 film sets, 4 theaters with film visual effects and restaurants, bars and shops.
