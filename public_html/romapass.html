Roma Pass

ROMA-PASS (three-day) = 36 euro
ROMA-PASS 48 hours (two-day) = 28 euro

Roman tourist cards ROMA-ROMA-PASS and PASS 48 hours, under certain conditions, can help in the smooth exploring the Eternal City. You have the right to count all the planned entry fees and costs, in order to understand whether you will pay off.

Let's do a brief account of the three-day card:


three-day ticket for public transport (BTI) = 16.50 euro
admission to the Colosseum-Forum-Palatine = 12 euro
admission to the Castle of St. Angel = 10.50

------------------- Total: 39 euro
Same as above, I have for 36 euros, buying a card ROMA-PASS (3 euros less)
However, if you live in the center of Rome and you will not use public transportation, this bill is simple ... ROMA-PASS does not pay you ...

Package card ROMA-PASS includes:

- Free admission to the first two visited national museums in Rome;
- Discounts for all subsequent state museums in Rome (for three days validity of the card);
- Free admission to some of the smaller museums: Museo della Repubblica Romana e della memoria garibaldine, Museo Bilotti in the Villa Borghese park, Museo Canonica, the Museo delle Mura Museo Napoleonico and Villa di Massenzio;
- Discounts on some exhibitions and performances in Rome;
- A three-day access to the means of transport ATAC (buses, trams, trolley buses, metro A and B metro lines underground terrestrial Roma-Pantano, Roma-Lido, Roma-Viterbo within the city of Rome).

Package card ROMA-PASS 48 hours includes:

- Free admission to the first state museum are visiting Rome; - Discounts for all subsequent state museums in Rome (for three days validity of the card);
- Free admission to some of the smaller museums: Museo della Repubblica Romana e della memoria garibaldine, Museo Bilotti in the Villa Borghese park, Museo Canonica, the Museo delle Mura Museo Napoleonico and Villa di Massenzio;
- Discounts on some exhibitions and performances in Rome;
- A two-day access to the means of transport ATAC (buses, trams, trolley buses, metro A and B metro lines underground terrestrial Roma-Pantano, Roma-Lido, Roma-Viterbo within the city of Rome).

Warning! Both cards do not include the Trenitalia railway and bus ATRAL / Cotral - eg. You can not on the cards to travel by train line FR 1-2-3-4-5, you can not get from / to Fiumicino and Ciampino airports.

Together with the card tourist receives:

- The map of the center of Rome
- Tourist information on museums and current cultural events.
Unfortunately, the Vatican Museums and other non-state tourist facilities are excluded from the ROMA PASS card!

PURCHASE OF ROME CARD TRAVEL ROMA-PASS
