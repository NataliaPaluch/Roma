One Day in Rome

Visiting Rome in one day - the route tours for those who want to honestly say that they were in Rome ...

Visiting Rome in one day, of course, a difficult issue. To complete all the most important places in the city in such a short time, you have to first of all have a large physical condition, because to reach the proposed sites requires movement on foot or using a fairly expensive service hiring transport with a driver. Getting around by public transport, in this case, it is completely meaningless. So if you do not have more time to Rome, then heels!

The beginning: Square and Basilica. Peter
End: Termini station
Length: 7.5 km
Time: 6/8 hours walk
Sightseeing Places:
1) Square and Basilica. Peter (1 hr. + 1 hr. On the dome of the basilica)
2) St. George's Castle. Angelo and the Bridge of Angels (outside)
3) Piazza Navona (Fountain of the Four Rivers)
4) The Pantheon (admission free)
5) Montecitorio Palace (Palace of the Italian parliament)
6) Column of Marcus Aurelius
7) Children's and so. Spanish Steps
8) Trevi Fountain
9) Presidential Palace Quirinale
10) Column of Trajan
11) Altar of the Fatherland - Children's Venetian
12) Capitol - City Hall
13) The observation deck at the Roman Forum
14) Street Imperial Forums
15) Colosseum
16) The observation deck at the Coliseum
17) Basilica of San Pietro in Vincoli ( "Moses" by Michelangelo)
18) The Basilica of Santa Maria Maggiore
